# Product register

## Product
`website-find-room` is a room-finding marketplace for renters and landlords. The product helps people discover rental rooms quickly, compare options confidently, and post listings with enough detail to drive real decisions.

## Users
- Renters who want to search by city, district, ward, price, area, room type, and amenities
- Landlords who want to create clear listings and attract qualified leads
- People who care about practical details more than visual novelty

## Product purpose
- Make it easy to search and compare rooms
- Surface the most relevant listings first
- Reduce uncertainty with images, VR, map context, and clear property details
- Support both browsing and posting flows without adding friction

## Voice
- Clear
- Practical
- Reassuring
- Direct

## Brand voice in three words
- Helpful
- Trustworthy
- Efficient

## Experience principles
- Familiar patterns beat clever ones
- Show only what helps the decision
- Keep filters understandable and predictable
- Prefer density where it helps scanning, but avoid clutter
- Make search results feel reliable and current

## Anti-references
- Overly decorative real-estate landing pages
- Heavy animation that slows task completion
- Generic SaaS hero sections with fake metrics
- Visual tricks that distract from listings and search
- Novel UI controls that make filtering harder to use

## Strategic notes
- The homepage should introduce the product, then hand off to search fast
- The rooms page is the core task surface and should feel like a useful tool
- The post flow should feel guided, calm, and confidence-building
